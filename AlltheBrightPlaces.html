All the Bright Places

Jennifer Niven

One of the books that I love is called All The Bright Places, by Jennifer Niven.




Synopsis:
Theodore and Violet are from very different groups in high school. Violet is pretty, popular, and grieving the loss of her older sister. Finch is weird, "a freak", and will disappear from school for long periods while he fights to stay alive. When they meet one day on the ledge of the school belltower, they save each other and develope an intense friendship that gets them through a roller coaster of events that leaves them shaken.

Warning
This book contains some mature themes that could be triggering for younger audiences, includng depression, suicide, parental abuse, family death, and sex. Despite it's mature themes and scenes, it is perfect for older teens. Common Sense Media rates both the book and the movie as 14+.
